Smart Retail Solutions

Retail is shifting from fragmented cameras to unified, data-driven operations. NE deploys Hikvision's retail lineup to help shops, supermarkets and malls see what's happening on the floor — and act on it.

People-counting and video analytics turn footfall into actionable insight, while ANPR and access control secure entrances and back-of-house.
