FAQ

Common questions about Retail security

What Hikvision cameras does NE recommend for a supermarket in Kenya?
NE typically pairs IP dome and turret cameras at entrances and shelving with ANPR cameras at the parking gate, all managed through HikCentral Professional for multi-store oversight.
Can Hikvision retail security help reduce shrinkage?
Yes. People-counting analytics, loss-prevention cameras at high-value shelving, and access control on back-of-house areas are the core tools Hikvision uses to cut retail shrinkage.
Does NE install retail CCTV systems or just sell the hardware?
NE's own technicians handle installation, system design and after-sales support — not just equipment supply.
