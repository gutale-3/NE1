FAQ

Common questions about Energy security

How does Hikvision protect a solar farm with no on-site staff?
Thermal cameras detect intrusions day or night in any weather, and radar-camera linkage automatically points nearby PTZ cameras at a detected threat.
Can solar farm security cameras run without mains power?
Yes. Hikvision's solar-powered cameras remove the need for additional wiring at remote sites.
What does remote inspection look like for a solar farm?
A HikCentral video-wall dashboard gives the operations team a bird's-eye view of the whole site, cutting the need for physical site visits.
