FAQ

Common questions about Education security

How does Hikvision prevent a child from being left on a school bus?
A PIR sensor detects a student still onboard after the bus empties and automatically alerts the driver and control center — no manual headcount required.
Can Hikvision automate student attendance?
Yes. Facial recognition access control at the gate or classroom door logs attendance automatically as students pass through.
What does a Hikvision campus security system typically cover?
School bus, perimeter, school gate, campus grounds, control room and classroom — six zones that close blind spots on and off school hours.
What access control options work best for schools and campuses?
Facial recognition or card-based access at gates and classroom doors is the most common setup — it logs entry automatically, doubles as attendance tracking, and can restrict access to specific buildings or times of day for visitors and contractors.
What does a typical campus CCTV setup include?
Coverage of the school gate, perimeter fence, school bus, campus grounds, corridors and a control room monitor — sized to the number of buildings and entrances on site, with footage retained long enough to review any incident.
