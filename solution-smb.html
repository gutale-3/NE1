FAQ

Common questions about Small Business security

What's included in a Hikvision small business security kit?
Typically an NVR, cameras, access control and an alarm system sized to the site — matched to a store, office or home rather than an enterprise deployment.
Can I monitor my shop or home remotely with Hikvision?
Yes, via the Hik-Connect app, which supports both personal (single site) and team (multi-site) management modes.
Does Hikvision offer video intercom for small offices?
Yes — indoor and door stations with two-way audio and video secure front gates and office entrances.

Tell NE about your small business project and we'll recommend the right cameras, recorders and access control.
