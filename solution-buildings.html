FAQ

Common questions about Buildings security

How does Hikvision face recognition access control work in an apartment or office?
Residents or staff are granted access to authorized floors or units simply by looking at a face-recognition terminal, which can integrate directly with elevator control.
Can Hikvision handle hotel guest experience, not just security?
Yes — digital valet parking, joyful check-in, lobby digital signage and smart hotel locks are all part of Hikvision's Smart Hotel Solution.
What is a Hikvision panic alarm station?
A wall-mounted button that routes an emergency straight to a central master station for a fast staff response.
