FAQ

Common questions about Logistics & Warehousing security

What cameras does Hikvision use for warehouse dock monitoring?
Fisheye panoramic cameras give a wide, single-camera view of loading and unloading areas, cutting the number of cameras needed per dock.
Can Hikvision speed up vehicle entry at a warehouse gate?
Yes. ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) cameras authenticate registered vehicles automatically, removing manual gate checks.
Does Hikvision warehouse security include fire and temperature detection?
Yes, alongside perimeter, entrance and public-area coverage as part of a full warehouse security layout.
